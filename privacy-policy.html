Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26 Oct 2024

Welcome to The Car Wash, We value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect you data when you visit our website, use our services, or communicate with us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from you:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, and payment information. We collect this data when you book a service, create an account, or communicate with us.
  • Usage Information: We gather information about your use of our website, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This data helps us improve our website and services.
  • Location Information: With your consent, we may collect location data to suggest nearby car wash locations or services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and manage our car wash services
  • Process transactions and send you confirmation emails
  • Improve our website, products, and customer service
  • Send promotional offers or updates (if you have opted in)
  • Respond to inquiries and offer customer support

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to:

  • Process payments securely through third-party payment processors
  • Comply with legal obligations or protect our rights
  • Collaborate with trusted service providers who help us manage our business

4. Security of Your Information

We use a variety of industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption and secure servers. However, no system can be completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection of your data.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and personalize content. You can modify your cookie settings in your browser, though this may limit some website functionality.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of promotional communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request restrictions on certain uses of your data

Please contact us using the details below if you wish to exercise any of these rights.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the latest update will be indicated at the top of the policy.
